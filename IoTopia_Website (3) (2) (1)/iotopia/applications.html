Healthcare IoT

IoT is revolutionising patient care by enabling continuous monitoring, faster diagnoses and more personalised treatment — often from the patient's home.

$534BMarket by 2028
40%Cost Reduction
76MRemote Patients
  • ❤️
    Remote Patient Monitoring

    Wearable sensors track heart rate, blood pressure and oxygen levels, alerting clinicians to changes in real time.

  • 💊
    Smart Medication Dispensers

    Connected dispensers remind patients to take medications and alert caregivers if doses are missed.

  • 🏃
    Rehabilitation Tracking

    Motion sensors track patient progress during physiotherapy, providing clinicians with objective recovery data.

  • 🔬
    Hospital Asset Tracking

    RFID and Bluetooth tags locate medical equipment, reducing search time by up to 70%.

Industrial IoT (IIoT)

IIoT connects machines, sensors and systems on factory floors, enabling unprecedented levels of efficiency and automation.

$1.1TMarket by 2028
25%Productivity Gain
50%Maintenance Cut
  • 🔧
    Predictive Maintenance

    Vibration and temperature sensors detect machine wear before failure, preventing costly downtime.

  • 📦
    Supply Chain Tracking

    Real-time GPS and RFID tracking of goods from manufacturer to consumer.

  • 🤖
    Autonomous Robotics

    IoT-connected robots work alongside humans in warehouses with real-time coordination.

  • ⚡
    Energy Management

    Smart meters monitor energy consumption across a facility and automatically reduce waste.

Smart Agriculture IoT

Precision agriculture uses IoT sensors and analytics to help farmers make smarter decisions, reduce waste and grow more with fewer resources.

40%Water Reduction
15%Yield Increase
$22BAgriTech Market
  • 💧
    Smart Irrigation

    Soil moisture sensors trigger irrigation only when needed, cutting water consumption by up to 40%.

  • 🌤️
    Microclimate Monitoring

    Field-level weather stations provide hyper-local data to optimise planting decisions.

  • 🐄
    Livestock Monitoring

    GPS collars and biosensors track animal health, location and behaviour across large farms.

  • 🚁
    Drone Surveillance

    IoT-connected drones survey crop areas and identify disease or irrigation issues early.
