How It Works

The IoT Ecosystem in 4 Steps

Every IoT system follows the same fundamental flow — from physical sensing to intelligent action.

1

Sense

Physical sensors collect real-world data — temperature, motion, pressure and more.

2

Connect

Data is transmitted via Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, 5G or LPWAN to the cloud or local gateway.

3

Process

Cloud or edge computing analyses the data, often using AI and machine learning algorithms.

4

Act

Insights trigger automated responses — adjusting systems, sending alerts or informing decisions.
