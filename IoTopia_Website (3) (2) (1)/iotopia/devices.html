Sensors

Common IoT Sensors

Sensors are the eyes and ears of IoT systems — here are the six most commonly used types.

🌡️
Environmental

Temperature & Humidity

DHT11, DHT22 and BME280 sensors measure ambient conditions. Essential for smart home and agricultural applications.

📡
Motion

PIR Motion Sensors

Passive infrared sensors detect people by measuring infrared radiation changes. Used in security and smart lighting.

🌊
Soil & Water

Soil Moisture Sensors

Capacitive and resistive sensors measure soil water content for precision irrigation systems in agriculture.

❤️
Health

Pulse Oximeter

MAX30102 sensors measure blood oxygen saturation (SpO2) and heart rate for wearable health monitoring.

💨
Air Quality

Gas & Air Quality

MQ-series sensors detect CO₂, methane and carbon monoxide for safety and environmental monitoring.

📏
Industrial

Ultrasonic Distance

HC-SR04 sensors use sound waves to measure distance — used in parking sensors and tank level monitoring.
